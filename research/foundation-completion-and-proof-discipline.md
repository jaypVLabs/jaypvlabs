# Foundation Completion and Proof Discipline

**Status:** jaypVLabs research record

## Core Finding

The Recursive Accessibility and Balance framework should not expand by assumption.

It must expand by disciplined derivation.

## Current Compression

```text
Accessibility explains what can happen.
Balance explains what can continue.
```

## Proof Discipline

No concept becomes foundational until every simpler explanation has been eliminated or shown insufficient.

Working method:

1. Observe.
2. Distinguish.
3. Define.
4. Test.
5. Reject or retain.
6. Repeat.

## Items Requiring Clarification

Clarify whether distinguishability is fundamental or derived.

### Balance

### Measurement

Clarify how universal accessibility becomes domain-specific measurement.

### Language Authority

Create one controlled vocabulary so the framework does not fragment across mathematics, philosophy, engineering, governance, and implementation.
